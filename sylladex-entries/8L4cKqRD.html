About / Description

holy goated album. i remember first listening to my chemical romance after a long english lesson in like year 10. i listened to all of bullets like 6 times in a row and was just like in love. at the time i thought the black parade was a super corny album. but then i think it was a very cold winters night i was sat at my computer and i just sat there and forced myself to listen to the whole album. and ever since, ive loved it!

ok now the story behind this cassette! i had been cassette hunting for a few months now, and i REALLY wanted like ANYTHING mcr but i could genuinely find nothing! like damn i know mcr is an american band but i thought i would find SOMETHING. eventually i started looking online for stuff and thats when i came across a listing for the black parade on cassette. it was a lot of money. more than i really had in spending money at the time. but after my second paycheck i just thought fuck it and i bought the thing. a few weeks later my mum was knocking on my bedroom door with a package. i opened it right there and then, she asked me how much it was and i got a stern talking to about how i shouldnt impluse buy like that. i SWEAR i dont normally do that but like i dont even care its a beautiful cassette.
