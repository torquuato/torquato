About / Description

this is my PS VITA. i got this bad boy a while ago when i was really into danganronpa. like i seriously only got this thing initially to play the danganronpa games and also parapa the rapper (which i didnt know at the time was only on the psp). after finding out that i couldnt play parapa the rapper i kinda got bored of the thing and it would sit in my desk drawer for several years.

sometime this year (somewhere around like june-july 2025) i found it again while cleaning my room and went on the interwebs to see what games i could get on it. this is how i discovered the psvita hombrew scene! it is INCREDIBLY easy to mod your PSVITA and you can fully just do it completely in your vita with no need for a computer and such.

here is a useful link you can use as a starting point: here. im pretty sure this is the exact video i watched to do my vita.

getting into the real meat of the vita, i REALLY like silent hill origins. it was the first silent hill game i properly played and imo, for the vita it was SUPER impressive and cool. ive seen playthroughs of silent hill 2 and i really got that vibe walking around the town and buildings which i fully wasnt expecting for the vita (especially after playing book of memories). danganronpa, i loved ages ago, and i loved again when i played it on my vita. SOME games crashed my vita a bunch but there is some settings in the homebrew file menu you can change to minimise this im pretty sure. i think that the homebrew browser is super cool and epic. there are so many brilliant game ports such as crazy cattle 3d.

100% would reccomend getting a psvita and slapping some homebrew in that thang.
