About / Description

this was one of my first casssettes in my collection! i was at a carboot sale and saw a massive box filled with cassettes and vinyls. i rummaged through and found this cassette between a motley crue cassette and a richard sinclair 'caravan of dreams' cassette (both of which i purchased as well).

listening to this cassette was the first time i ever really sat down and listened to this album. i was sat on my own waiting for a bus on a late winter evening with my cassette player and my phone was dead so i couldnt listen to spotify. so i decided to fully listen to this cassette back to front and realised that it was pretty alright.

songs i never heard of prior that i actualy really liked? polly and territorial pissings. i was surprised that there were songs that i hadnt even heard of on this album but it was really nice surprise.
